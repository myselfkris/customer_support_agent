ACME CORP — SHIPPING POLICY
Version 1.4 | Effective Date: January 1, 2026

===========================================================
SECTION 1: STANDARD SHIPPING
===========================================================

Acme Corp offers the following standard shipping options for all domestic
(United States) orders:

Standard Shipping (5-7 business days): FREE on orders over $50. $4.99 for
orders under $50.

Expedited Shipping (2-3 business days): $12.99 flat rate for all orders.

Overnight Shipping (next business day): $24.99 flat rate. Orders must be
placed before 12:00 PM EST to qualify for next-day delivery. Orders placed
after 12:00 PM EST will ship the following business day.

===========================================================
SECTION 2: ORDER PROCESSING TIME
===========================================================

All orders are processed within 1 to 2 business days of being placed.
Orders placed on weekends or federal holidays will be processed on the
next business day.

Once your order is processed and shipped, you will receive a shipping
confirmation email with a tracking number. Allow 24 hours for the tracking
number to become active on the carrier's website.

===========================================================
SECTION 3: SHIPPING CARRIERS
===========================================================

Acme Corp ships via the following carriers depending on package size and
destination:

- UPS: Standard and expedited domestic orders
- FedEx: Overnight and large item orders
- USPS: Small packages and rural delivery areas

You cannot select a specific carrier. Acme Corp reserves the right to
choose the most efficient carrier for each shipment.

===========================================================
SECTION 4: INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING
===========================================================

Acme Corp ships to over 45 countries. International shipping rates and
estimated delivery times vary by destination:

Canada: 7-14 business days, starting at $14.99
United Kingdom: 10-18 business days, starting at $19.99
Australia: 14-21 business days, starting at $22.99
European Union: 10-16 business days, starting at $17.99

All international orders may be subject to customs duties and import taxes.
These charges are the responsibility of the customer and are NOT included
in the shipping price. Acme Corp is not responsible for delays caused by
customs processing.

===========================================================
SECTION 5: DELIVERY ISSUES
===========================================================

Lost Packages:
If your tracking shows your package has not moved in 5 or more business
days, contact our support team. We will open a carrier investigation.
If the package is confirmed lost, Acme Corp will reship the order or
issue a full refund at no cost to the customer.

Stolen Packages:
Acme Corp is not responsible for packages confirmed as delivered by the
carrier. If a delivered package is missing, file a report with local
authorities and contact your homeowner's or renter's insurance provider.
As a courtesy, we may offer store credit on a case-by-case basis.

Wrong Address:
If you provided an incorrect shipping address and the order has already
shipped, Acme Corp cannot guarantee address changes. Contact support
immediately and we will attempt to intercept the package, though this
is not guaranteed. Additional shipping charges may apply for reshipping.
