ACME CORP — RETURN & REFUND POLICY
Version 2.1 | Effective Date: January 1, 2026

===========================================================
SECTION 1: STANDARD RETURN POLICY
===========================================================

Acme Corp accepts returns within 30 days of the original purchase date.
Items must be in their original, unused condition with all original packaging,
tags, and accessories included. Proof of purchase is required for all returns.

To initiate a return, customers must:
1. Contact our support team at support@acmecorp.com or call 1-800-ACME-123
2. Receive a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number
3. Ship the item back within 7 days of receiving the RMA number
4. Use the prepaid shipping label provided by Acme Corp

Refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days of receiving the returned item
at our warehouse. The refund will be credited to the original payment method.

===========================================================
SECTION 2: BLACK FRIDAY AND SALE ITEMS
===========================================================

Items purchased during Black Friday, Cyber Monday, or any promotional sale event
are eligible for store credit only. Cash refunds and original payment method refunds
are NOT available for sale items. Store credit does not expire and can be used on
any future purchase.

Black Friday sale items may be exchanged within 14 days of purchase if the item
is defective or damaged upon arrival. Exchange requests must include photo evidence
of the damage submitted to support@acmecorp.com within 48 hours of delivery.

===========================================================
SECTION 3: SPECIAL PAYMENT METHODS
===========================================================

Gift Card Purchases:
Items purchased using a gift card are eligible for store credit only.
Refunds cannot be returned to a gift card. If a gift card was used as partial
payment, the refund will be split: the gift card portion returns as store credit,
and any credit card portion returns to the original credit card.

Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL):
For purchases made through Klarna, Afterpay, or similar BNPL services, refunds
are processed back to the BNPL provider. The provider's timeline for crediting
your account may vary from 5 to 14 business days.

PayPal Purchases:
Refunds for PayPal purchases are returned to the PayPal account within 3 to 5
business days after our warehouse receives the returned item.

===========================================================
SECTION 4: DAMAGED OR DEFECTIVE ITEMS
===========================================================

If you received a damaged or defective item, Acme Corp will provide:
- A full refund to the original payment method, OR
- A free replacement shipped within 2 business days

To report a damaged or defective item:
1. Take clear photos of the damage within 48 hours of delivery
2. Email the photos to damage@acmecorp.com with your order number
3. Our team will respond within 24 hours with resolution options

You do NOT need to return damaged items unless specifically requested by our team.

===========================================================
SECTION 5: NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS
===========================================================

The following items cannot be returned under any circumstances:
- Perishable goods (food items, flowers)
- Downloadable software or digital products once activated
- Customized or personalized items (engraved products, custom prints)
- Hygiene products (earbuds, face masks) once opened
- Gift cards (once purchased, gift cards cannot be refunded)

===========================================================
SECTION 6: INTERNATIONAL RETURNS
===========================================================

Customers outside the United States are responsible for return shipping costs.
International returns must be initiated within 14 days of delivery (not purchase).
Customs fees and import duties paid by the customer are non-refundable.
International refunds may take 10 to 15 business days due to currency conversion
and international banking processing times.
